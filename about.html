About Me

I’m Enrique — a 3D & 2D Designer and Creative Developer passionate about turning ideas into immersive visual experiences.

My work blends artistic vision with technical precision. From modeling stylized 3D props and hand-painted textures to building clean front-end interfaces, I aim to bring balance between creativity and functionality.

Based in the Canary Islands, I’m constantly exploring the intersection between design, technology, and storytelling — creating visuals and digital projects that feel cohesive, detailed, and meaningful.
